Blender
Caroline Cullen


Description

Like Slender but with a Blender.

Objective of the Game

  • Collect all da fruits
  • Bring em back to Blenderman
  • Reach another level of existence with you and all your adult friends
  • DONT GET ROOSTER BLOCKED

Controls

  • WASD to move
  • Mouse pointer to look around
  • Left-Shift to sprint

Features

  • Collision detection with edges of map and fruits
  • Random generation of fruit and tree positions
  • Trippy skysphere with layer textures that are interpolated based on "sun" position
  • Skysphere rotation
  • Rotating sun position
  • Billboarding and randomly generating particles
  • Multiple shape object uploads

Collision detection with edges of map and fruits

Before I incremented the camera position on each render call I checked if the magnitude of the distance between the camera position and either the fruit or the edge of the map was less than a certain radius. If the distance between the edge and the camera position was smaller than a certain amount I rendered the rooster and didn't increment the camera position so the user doesn't move. For the fruit if the distance was smaller then I changed the "collected" bool for that fruit and then no longer rendered it.


Random generation of fruit, particle and tree positions

Randomly generated positions for the 3 above features. In the case of tree and fruit these are generated before the render loop. In the case of the particle they are update random values for every render call.


Rotating skysphere position as light source

The skysphere is slowly rotating about the Y axis while the light source is rotating as well, which is updating the light vector being passed into the shaders where I am using phong shading, and thusly the "sun" rotation is changing the shading of my objects


Trippy skysphere with layer textures

The skysphere has two textures that are being interpolated and rendered based on the light position.

Billboarding and randomly generating particles

The particles always fact the user using the Billboarding technique. They are given random XYZ position, XYZ velocity, and lifespan

Multiple shape object uploads

Objects made of many different shapes and most are shaded differently.
